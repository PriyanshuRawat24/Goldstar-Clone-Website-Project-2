gold star battery (u) limited

We are one of the largest battery manufacturers in Uganda. Gold Star is a reputable and reliable battery manufacturer. We offer a variety of cutting-edge solutions for UPS and Solar appilcations. Our batteries are designed to offer maximum satisfaction to our customers and fulfill the energy requirements of their homes and solar power backup.

With longer backup, zero maintenance and smooth performance, our batteries are the ideal choice to power your life. We at Gold Star, always put more focus on being a responsible organization and therefore undertake steps to bring social sustainability and use the resources judiciously.

We constantly innovate and carry out Research & Development to bring quality production to the market. We establish long lasting relations with our channel partners and millions of consumers with our services.
